Regulatory Intelligence

Bureaucracy Simplified

Portugal's automotive tax landscape is shifting. We ensure you stay compliant and benefit from every reform.

2025 ISV Reform

Reductions of up to 60% on used EU imports under the new Table D rules. Eligible PHEVs receive up to 75% discounts on registration tax.

2026-2027 IUC Calendar Shift

The IUC payment date is consolidating from the month of registration to a single national deadline. This creates a potential "double payment" scenario for owners transitioning between systems.

  • 2026: Deadline moves to 28 February. Two installments available if over €100.
  • 2027: Final alignment to 30 April. Three installments available if over €500.

Common Questions About Our Services

Q: Do you handle car storage for expats who travel frequently?
A: Yes, our preservation protocol is specifically designed for expats who leave Portugal for extended periods. We provide battery maintenance, mechanical cycling, and regular inspections to ensure your car is ready when you return.

Q: Can you help import my car to Portugal?
A: Absolutely. Our Legalizer package handles all aspects of vehicle importation including DAV registration, COC documentation, ISV tax calculations, IPO Type B inspections, and final Portuguese license plate delivery.

Q: What is IPO and how often is it required?
A: IPO (Inspeção Periódica Obrigatória) is Portugal's mandatory vehicle inspection. New cars require inspection after 4 years, then every 2 years until 8 years old, then annually. We handle the entire process including pre-checks and transportation.

Q: Do you work with all car makes and models?
A: Yes, we service all vehicle types including gasoline, diesel, hybrid (PHEV), and electric vehicles (EVs). We're experienced with European, American, and Asian automotive brands.
